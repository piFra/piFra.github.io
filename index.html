Overthewire.org Bandit wargame

A post about learning the basics of wargames :D

Abstract

Yesterday while reading about SANS coin backstory I thought about hacker wargames that are so popular right now. Since I'm passionate about Computer Security and hacking in general, I decided to give some wargame a try. Where to start? Which game to choose? I searched a bit over the network, using the mystic oracle called GOOGLE. I found some nice answers, and and many of them said that the "Bandit" wargame from overthewire.org was a great starting point. So I said, why no? Let's try! In this post I want to share my progress, my thoughts, my errors and offer some discussion points about the levels. TL:DR; It' a really funny wargame! Congratulations overthewire! By the way it started from the basics and went trough some interesting concepts, like openSSL and SSH shells.
